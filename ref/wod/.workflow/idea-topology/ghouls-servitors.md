## CATALOG

[N600] Ghoul ◆ {V20} — Mortal fed vampiric vitae: gains Potence 1, blood pool, slowed aging, blood bond to domitor. Three types: Vassal (bonded servant), Independent (free agent), Revenant (hereditary). ✶?
[N601] Vassal ◆ {V20} — Standard ghoul: blood-bonded to a domitor vampire. Loyal through supernatural addiction/love. Most common type. Often ignorant of wider Kindred society.
[N602] Independent ghoul ○ {V20} — Free ghoul: obtains vitae without a master. Kidnaps vampires, buys blood, or was abandoned. Desperate, resourceful, hunted.
[N603] Revenant ◆ {V20} — Hereditary ghoul: family bred for centuries by Tzimisce. Generate own vitae (1 BP/day). Born ghouls. Always Sabbat-aligned. Universally disturbed psychologically. ✶?

### Revenant Families

[N610] Bratovitch ◆ {V20} — Hellhound breeders: rural, bestial, violent. Disciplines: Animalism, Fortitude, Vicissitude. Weakness: frenzy +2 difficulty, poor social skills with mortals.
[N611] Ducheski ◆ {V20} — Machine-makers: serve Tremere (betrayed Tzimisce). Mechanical geniuses, Da Vinci-like but grim. Disciplines: Auspex, Dominate, Thaumaturgy. Weakness: no Social Trait above 2, aberrant personalities.
[N612] Grimaldi ◆ {V20} — Social manipulators: "most normal" family. Italian merchants, political go-betweens. Disciplines: Celerity, Dominate, Fortitude. Weakness: blood-bonded to Sabbat Bishop+ past puberty.
[N613] Obertus ◆ {V20} — Reclusive scholars: research-obsessed, originally Byzantine librarians. Disciplines: Auspex, Obfuscate, Vicissitude. Weakness: OCD/monomania derangement. Secret goal: prove revenants are next evolutionary step.
[N614] Oprichniki ◆ {V20} — Russian warriors: descended from Ivan the Terrible's mercenaries. Serve Russian Tzimisce. Disciplines: Animalism, Obfuscate, Vicissitude. Weakness: ALL haunted by ghosts (3pt Supernatural Flaw).
[N615] Zantosa ◆ {V20} — Decadent hedonists: Eastern European pleasure-seekers. Disciplines: Auspex, Presence, Vicissitude. Weakness: weak-willed, addicted to sensation (Willpower roll to resist new pleasures). Guard a torpid Tzimisce elder.

### War Ghouls

[N630] Szlachta ◆ {V20} — Fleshcrafted Tzimisce minions: lobotomized ghouls warped by Vicissitude into monstrous combatants. Obey only. Can't rejoin society. Fortitude 1, Potence 1.
[N631] Vozhd ◆ {V20} — War-behemoths: 15+ ghouls grafted together via Vicissitude + Thaumaturgy. Walking weapons of mass destruction. Fortitude 4, Potence 6. Lobotomized = immune to Animalism, Dominate, Presence. Rarely used (Masquerade risk). ✶?

### Ghoul Rules

[N640] Ghoul blood bond ○ {V20} — Gradual (not sudden like vampire). Two drinks = Vinculum 5 equivalent. Three drinks for mortals = full bond. Ghouls with Willpower 5+ need Self-Control to resist third drink.
[N641] Ghoul aging ○ {V20} — Immune while fed monthly. Miss a month: resume normal aging. 100-250 years: age at 10x rate. 250+: crumble to dust without vitae. ✶?
[N642] Ghoul Discipline limits ○ {V20} — Capped by domitor's Generation: 13th-8th = Level 1, 7th = 2, 6th = 3, 5th = 4, 4th = 5. Revenants use own vitae for any Discipline.
[N643] Ghoul frenzy ○ {V20} — Lower difficulty than vampires (typically -3). Beast is weaker but ghouls untrained in managing it.
[N644] Ghoul Clan weaknesses ○ {V20} — Contagious from certain Clans: Brujah blood = frenzy matching vampire difficulty, Setite = sunlight rash, Malkavian = mild derangement, Nosferatu = Appearance drop, Ventrue = marked feeding preference.
[N645] Ghoul withdrawal ○ {V20} — (6 - Stamina) weeks of symptoms. Must Self-Control (diff 7) when vitae available or attempt to "score." Sublimation into other cravings (blood, flesh, sex) on failed Intelligence + Self-Control rolls.
[N646] Ghoul overdose ○ {V20} — Can "cram" extra BP (1 per Stamina). Excess = Stamina check or lethal damage + vomiting. Overdosed ghoul: frenzy chance = vampire's, reaction boost, hallucination risk.
