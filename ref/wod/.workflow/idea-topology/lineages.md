[N110] Toreador ◆ {V20} — Artist-aesthetes. Disciplines: Auspex, Celerity, Presence. Weakness: aesthetic fixation (Willpower or Self-Control to break free from beauty). ↔ N209

[N112] Tzimisce ◆ {V20} — Flesh-shaping lords, Sabbat scholars. Disciplines: Animalism, Auspex, Vicissitude. Weakness: must sleep with 2 handfuls native soil or lose 1 die/day. ↔ N211
[N113] Ventrue ◆ {V20} — Aristocrat rulers, Camarilla backbone. Disciplines: Dominate, Fortitude, Presence. Weakness: can only feed from specific mortal type (chosen at creation). ↔ N212
