## CATALOG

[N420] Gothic-Punk aesthetic ○ {V20} — World of Darkness visual/tonal frame: darker, meaner version of our world. Corruption deeper, nights longer, architecture more oppressive. Punk resistance against gothic decay. ✶?
[N421] The Masquerade (setting axiom) ○ {V20} — Vampires MUST hide from humanity. If discovered, humanity would exterminate them through sheer numbers. The Masquerade is survival, not tradition. ↔ N211
[N422] The Jyhad ◆ {V20} — Eternal shadow war among Methuselahs and Antediluvians: ancient vampires manipulate younger ones as pawns in millennia-long schemes. Everything is someone's move. Every event has a hidden purpose. ✶?
[N423] Caine mythology ◆ {V20} — Origin story: all vampires descend from Caine (biblical), cursed by God for murdering Abel. Embraced the 2nd Generation who Embraced 13 Antediluvians (3rd Gen). Book of Nod records this history. May be literally true, may be mythology.
[N424] The Antediluvians ◆ {V20} — 13 third-Generation vampires: founders of the Clans. Most in torpor. Some may be dead. Their awakening = Gehenna. Objects of worship (Sabbat: enemy, Camarilla: denied). ↔ N425
[N425] Gehenna ◆ {V20} — Vampiric apocalypse: the Antediluvians awaken and consume their descendants. Prophesied in the Book of Nod. Signs: thin blood, Red Star, Week of Nightmares. May be imminent. ✶?
[N426] Book of Nod ◆ {V20} — Vampiric holy text: fragmentary, contradictory accounts of Caine, the Antediluvians, and the prophecy of Gehenna. No complete copy exists. Core of Noddist scholarship.
[N427] Generation system (setting axiom) ○ {V20} — Distance from Caine determines power ceiling. Hardcoded hierarchy: elders WILL ALWAYS be more powerful than neonates. Diablerie = only upward mobility. ↔ N309
[N428] Embrace (setting axiom) ○ {V20} — Vampiric reproduction: drain mortal, feed blood back. Creates blood bond to sire. Requires Willpower expenditure. New vampire = one Generation higher than sire.
[N429] The Curse (setting axiom) ○ {V20} — Vampiric vulnerabilities: sunlight (lethal/aggravated), fire (aggravated), staking (paralysis, not death), decapitation (Final Death). Running water aversion (some). Garlic repulsion (some). Cross vulnerability (some). Holy water (burns at True Faith).
[N430] Final Death ○ {V20} — True permanent death: fire, sunlight, decapitation, heart removal + decap. Vampires are otherwise functionally immortal. The THREAT of Final Death drives all Kindred politics. …
[N431] Kindred lexicon ○ {V20} — Vampiric terminology: Kindred/Cainite (vampire), Kine (mortals), Lick (slang), Prince/Sire/Childe/Fledgling/Neonate/Ancilla/Elder/Methuselah. Shapes in-character communication. …

World rules the game assumes ○
│  | These are the INVARIANTS — things true for every chronicle,
│  | every character, every Sect. The physics of the World of Darkness.
│  | ↣ interesting: the axioms encode the GENRE. Change an axiom and you
│  |   change what kind of story you're telling.
│
├──→s Vampires are secret ○ {V20}
│   │  | The Masquerade isn't just a Camarilla law — it's an ecological necessity.
│   │  | Humanity outnumbers vampires millions-to-one. Discovery = extinction.
│   │  | ↣ interesting: the predator is PREY if unmasked. The most dangerous
│   │  |   creatures in the world live in constant fear of their food.
│   │
│   └── ✶ Setting where vampires are KNOWN: Masquerade broken △
│          | What happens the night after? Government response, vampire-hunting
│          | industry, vampire civil rights, blood farming.
│          | ↣ interesting: the genre shifts from horror to political thriller
│          | ⊕ prior art: True Blood, VtM: Bloodlines endings, some V5 scenarios
│
├──→s Vampires are hierarchical by blood ○ {V20}
│   │  | Generation = power ceiling. Older = stronger. Period.
│   │  | ↣ interesting: an UNCHALLENGEABLE hierarchy. Not earned — inherited.
│   │  |   You're powerful because your sire was powerful.
│   │  |   Meritocracy is impossible in a Generation-based system.
│   │  |   (Unless you commit the ultimate crime: diablerie.)
│   │
│   └── ✶ Flat-Generation setting: all vampires at same Generation ▲
│          | No inherent hierarchy. Power comes ONLY from age, skill, politics.
│          | ↣ interesting: removes the "born better" axis — pure meritocracy for monsters
│          | ⊕ prior art: some one-shot scenarios, Requiem's weaker generation system
│
├──→s Vampires are cursed (Caine mythology) ○ {V20}
│   │  | Biblical origin. The curse is REAL — sunlight, fire, the Beast.
│   │  | God's punishment made flesh.
│   │  | ↣ interesting: vampires are THEOLOGICALLY cursed. God exists in this world
│   │  |   (True Faith works). Being a vampire is being DAMNED. Not metaphorically.
│   │
│   ├──→s The Antediluvians ◆: sleeping godlike ancestors {V20}
│   │      | ↣ interesting: your great-great-grandfather is a SLEEPING GOD
│   │      |   who will wake up and EAT you. Genealogy as eschatology.
│   │
│   └──→s Gehenna ◆: prophesied apocalypse {V20}
│          | ↣ interesting: vampires have their OWN apocalypse, separate from human
│          |   end-times. The world might end FOR VAMPIRES while humans carry on.
│          ├── ✶ Post-Gehenna setting: Antediluvians woke, some Clans destroyed △
│          │      | The prophecy came TRUE. Now what? Survivor horror.
│          │      | ⊕ prior art: V:tM Gehenna sourcebook (exactly this)
│          └── ✶! False Gehenna: the prophecy was a CONTROL MECHANISM by Methuselahs ▲
│                 | Gehenna is a LIE used to keep younger vampires obedient.
│                 | ↣ interesting: religion as conspiracy — the Jyhad goes ALL the way down
│
├──→s Vampires are manipulated (the Jyhad) ○ {V20}
│      | Methuselahs and Antediluvians use younger vampires as chess pieces.
│      | Everything has a hidden purpose. Paranoia is justified.
│      | ↣ interesting: the Jyhad means NOTHING IS WHAT IT SEEMS. Every alliance,
│      |   every betrayal, every war might be someone else's plan.
│      |   Free will is the game's most precious and most doubtful commodity.
│      | ⊕ seen in: Illuminati conspiracy genre, but the puppet-masters are
│      |   literally YOUR ANCESTORS, making it personal
│
├──→s Gothic-Punk aesthetic ○ {V20}
│      | The world is darker, grittier, more corrupt than ours.
│      | Decaying grandeur meets street-level resistance.
│      | ↣ interesting: the AESTHETIC is a game mechanic — it tells you what
│      |   kind of scenes to frame, what the city looks like, how NPCs act.
│      |   Tone as system.
│

       | Three specific weaknesses that all vampires share.
       | ↣ interesting: these aren't arbitrary — they're CULTURALLY LOADED.
       |   Sunlight = God's judgment. Fire = purification. Stake = penetration
       |   of the heart. Every vulnerability is symbolic.
       └── ✶? Mechanical exploration of vampire weaknesses as culturally relative
              (what if Chinese Kindred fear different things than European ones?)
