[N503] Stamina ◆ {V20} — Endurance, resilience. Soak rolls, endurance.
[N504] Charisma ◆ {V20} — Natural charm, social magnetism. First impressions, leadership.
[N505] Manipulation ◆ {V20} — Ability to influence others. Persuasion, deception, command.

[N520] Backgrounds system ○ {V20} — 11 social/situational advantages: Allies, Contacts, Domain, Fame, Generation, Herd, Influence, Mentor, Resources, Retainers, Status. Rated 0-5. Represent social capital, not personal ability. ✶?
[N521] Allies ◆ {V20} — Mortal friends/associates who help voluntarily. Not blood-bound.
[N522] Contacts ◆ {V20} — Information sources across society. One major + minor per dot.
[N523] Domain ◆ {V20} — Claimed territory for feeding/dwelling. Size/quality by dots.
[N524] Fame ◆ {V20} — Public recognition (as mortal identity). Benefits AND Masquerade risks.
[N525] Generation (Background) ◆ {V20} — Starting Generation below 13th. 1 dot = 12th, 5 dots = 8th. ↔ N309
[N526] Herd ◆ {V20} — Reliable feeding pool: people who willingly (or unknowingly) provide blood.
[N527] Influence ◆ {V20} — Pull in mortal institutions: government, police, media, business.
[N528] Mentor ◆ {V20} — Elder vampire guide/patron. Advice, resources, protection.
[N529] Resources ◆ {V20} — Wealth and material assets. Monthly income, property.
[N530] Retainers ◆ {V20} — Loyal servants: ghouls, mortal employees. Not independent — directed by you.
[N531] Status ◆ {V20} — Standing within vampiric society. Recognized authority.

## TREE (Collapsed — >50 nodes)

```
Character mechanical identity ○
│  | How a character is DESCRIBED in game terms.
│  | Three layers: innate (Attributes), learned (Abilities), contextual (Backgrounds + Merits/Flaws).
│
├──→s Attributes ○: what you ARE (9 traits, 3 categories) {V20}
│   ├──→v Physical: Strength / Dexterity / Stamina
│   ├──→v Social: Charisma / Manipulation / Appearance
│   │      | ↣ interesting: APPEARANCE is a stat. Beauty has a number. Nosferatu
│   │      |   have 0, meaning they are mechanically INCAPABLE of being attractive.
│   │      |   Social inequality is hardcoded.
│   └──→v Mental: Perception / Intelligence / Wits
│
├──→s Abilities ○: what you've LEARNED (30 traits, 3 categories) {V20}
│   ├──→v Talents: intuitive (Alertness, Athletics, Brawl, Empathy, Expression,
│   │      Intimidation, Leadership, Streetwise, Subterfuge, Awareness) …
│   ├──→v Skills: practiced (Animal Ken, Crafts, Drive, Etiquette, Firearms,
│   │      Larceny, Melee, Performance, Stealth, Survival) …
│   └──→v Knowledges: studied (Academics, Computer, Finance, Investigation,
│          Law, Medicine, Occult, Politics, Science, Technology) …
│
├──→s Backgrounds ○: what your SITUATION provides (11 traits) {V20}
│      | ↣ interesting: Backgrounds are SOCIAL CAPITAL. A vampire with Resources 5
│      |   and Status 5 is more dangerous than one with all 5s in combat stats.
│      |   V:tM mechanizes PRIVILEGE.
│      └── ✶? Background that represents mortal OBLIGATIONS (job, family, debts)
│             — costs for maintaining a mortal cover identity
│
└──→s Merits & Flaws ○: unique individual traits (~75) {V20}
    │  | Optional point-buy advantages and disadvantages.
    │  | ↣ interesting: Flaws GIVE you points — being disadvantaged makes you
    │  |   MORE capable in other areas. Disability-as-advantage, mechanically.
    │
    ├──→v Physical: body-based traits (Huge Size, Eat Food, Blind, etc.)
    ├──→v Mental: mind-based traits (Iron Will, Eidetic Memory, Flashbacks, etc.)
    ├──→v Social: status-based traits (Prestigious Sire, Red List, Clan Friendship, etc.)
    │      | ↣ interesting: social Flaws are often MORE interesting than Merits.
    │      |   "Former Prince" (3pt Flaw) tells an entire story in two words.
    └──→v Supernatural: paranormal traits (True Faith, Nine Lives, Dark Fate, etc.)
           | ↣ interesting: True Faith (7pt Merit) is the most expensive Merit
           |   in the game and the most thematically loaded — a VAMPIRE with FAITH.
           |   The damned who still believes. The contradiction is the character.
           └── ✶! Flaw that makes you INVISIBLE to supernatural detection ▲
                  | Not Obfuscate — a natural absence. Auspex can't see you.
                  | You don't register as vampire OR mortal. You're a null.
                  | ↣ interesting: identity void — what ARE you if no power can classify you?
```
