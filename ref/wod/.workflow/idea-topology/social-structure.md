### Camarilla Structure

[N210] The Six Traditions ◆ {V20} — Foundational laws: Masquerade, Domain, Progeny, Accounting, Hospitality, Destruction. Attributed to Caine. ✶?
[N211] First Tradition: The Masquerade ◆ {V20} — Hide vampiric existence from mortals. Violation = blood hunt. Core Camarilla law. ↔ N400
[N212] Second Tradition: Domain ◆ {V20} — A Prince's claimed territory. Authority over all within.
[N213] Third Tradition: Progeny ◆ {V20} — Must have permission to Embrace. Sire responsible for childe.
[N214] Fourth Tradition: Accounting ◆ {V20} — Sire responsible for childe until released by Prince.
[N215] Fifth Tradition: Hospitality ◆ {V20} — Must present yourself to the Prince of a domain you enter.
[N216] Sixth Tradition: Destruction ◆ {V20} — Only elders (Princes) may call the blood hunt (Lextalionis). Right of destruction over childer.
[N217] Inner Circle ◆ {V20} — Supreme governing body, meets once per century in Venice. One member per Clan. Appoints Justicars.
[N218] Justicar ◆ {V20} — Supreme judges, one per Clan. Enforce Traditions, arbitrate disputes. Served by Archons.
[N219] Archon ◆ {V20} — Agents of the Justicars. Field operatives and enforcers.
[N220] Prince ◆ {V20} — Ruler of a city/domain. Enforces Traditions locally. Not hereditary — seized/maintained by power.
[N221] Primogen ◆ {V20} — Council of Clan representatives advising the Prince. Varies by city.
[N222] Sheriff ◆ {V20} — Prince's enforcer. Polices domain, hunts violators.
[N223] Harpy ◆ {V20} — Social arbiter. Controls status, gossip, reputation.
[N224] Keeper of Elysium ◆ {V20} — Maintains Elysium (neutral gathering places). Enforces peace there.
[N225] Elysium ◆ {V20} — Neutral ground: no violence, no Disciplines, no destruction of art. Museums, galleries, clubs. ✶?
[N226] Scourge ◆ {V20} — Hunts unauthorized Embraces, especially thin-bloods and Caitiff.
[N227] Seneschal ◆ {V20} — Prince's right-hand; acts as Prince in absence.
[N228] Whip ◆ {V20} — Primogen's enforcer within their Clan.

### Sabbat Structure

[N230] Regent ◆ {V20} — Supreme leader of the Sabbat. Rules from Mexico City.
[N231] Cardinal ◆ {V20} — Rules a large geographic region. Appointed by Regent.
[N232] Priscus ◆ {V20} — Clan elder-advisors within Sabbat. Form the Prisci council. Advisory, not executive.
[N233] Archbishop ◆ {V20} — Rules a city for the Sabbat. Equivalent to Prince.
[N234] Bishop ◆ {V20} — Serves under Archbishop. Various administrative duties.
[N235] Pack ◆ {V20} — Basic Sabbat social unit (3-10 vampires). War coterie bonded by Vaulderie. ✶?
[N236] Ductus ◆ {V20} — Pack leader. War chief.
[N237] Pack Priest ◆ {V20} — Spiritual leader of pack. Conducts ritae. ↔ N260
[N238] Templar ◆ {V20} — Bodyguards for Sabbat leaders. "Paladins."
[N239] Code of Milan ◆ {V20} — Sabbat charter/constitution. Rules governing the Sect.

### Sabbat Ritae

[N260] Sabbat Ritae system ▲ {V20} — Formal rituals reinforcing pack loyalty and Sect identity. Two tiers: Auctoritas (mandatory/Sect-wide) and Ignoblis (local/pack). ↔ N237 ✶?
[N261] Auctoritas Ritae ◆ {V20} — Sect-wide rituals:
[N262] Blood Bath ◆ {V20} — Leadership recognition ceremony; new leader bathes in blood while subordinates pledge.
[N263] Blood Feast ◆ {V20} — Formal feeding event: captured mortals presented, bound, drained communally. Social gathering + feeding.
[N264] Creation Rites ◆ {V20} — Mass Embrace ritual: Embrace, bury, let fledglings claw free. "Shovelhead" method. Followed by Vaulderie to bind to pack.
[N265] Fire Dance ◆ {V20} — Courage-testing ritual: vampires dance around bonfire, leap through flames. Overcomes fire fear (Rötschreck).
[N266] Games of Instinct ◆ {V20} — Predatory games blessed by Pack Priest: Cowboys & Indians, Demolition Derby, Rat Race, Rousing the Beast.
[N267] Monomacy ◆ {V20} — Formal duel to settle disputes. Priest adjudicates. Often to Final Death.
[N268] Sermons of Caine ◆ {V20} — Book of Nod readings: recitation, meditation, theological debate.
[N269] War Party ◆ {V20} — Organized elder-hunt: packs compete to diablerise a target elder. Winner gains Sabbat Status.
[N270] Wild Hunt ◆ {V20} — Traitor execution: hunt and destroy Sabbat who betrayed Sect secrets. Extends to all who learned the secret.
[N271] Ignoblis Ritae ◆ {V20} — Local/pack rituals: Acceptance Ritus, Contrition Ritus, Stealth Ritus, Sun Dance. Vary by pack.
