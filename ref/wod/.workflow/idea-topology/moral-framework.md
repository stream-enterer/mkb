## CATALOG

[N350] Morality system ○ {V20} — Rated 1-10 scale measuring adherence to a moral code. Degeneration rolls when violating the code's hierarchy of sins. Failure = lose a point + risk derangement. Two systems: Humanity (default) and Paths of Enlightenment (alternative). ✶?
[N351] Humanity ◆ {V20} — Default morality: measures remaining human empathy. 10 = saintly, 7 = normal human, 4 = hardened criminal, 1 = barely sentient monster, 0 = Beast takes over (character lost). Higher Humanity = easier interaction with mortals, earlier waking, more human appearance.
[N352] Hierarchy of Sins ○ {V20} — Each morality level has a threshold sin. Committing sins at or below your rating triggers degeneration roll. Humanity sins: 10=selfish thoughts, 9=minor selfishness, 8=property damage, 7=theft, 6=accidental violation (killing in frenzy), 5=intentional property destruction, 4=passionate crime (manslaughter), 3=planned crime (murder), 2=casual crime (torture), 1=utter perversion.
[N353] Degeneration ○ {V20} — When a sin is committed: roll Conscience/Conviction (diff 8 - varies by Path). Failure = lose 1 morality point. Botch = also gain derangement. Success = keep current rating (character rationalizes or feels guilt).
[N354] Golconda ◆ {V20} — Mythical state of transcendence: vampire at peace with the Beast. No longer needs to fight frenzy. Possibly apocryphal. Requires high Humanity and mysterious quest. ✶?

### Paths of Enlightenment

[N355] Paths of Enlightenment system ▲ {V20} — Alternative morality codes for vampires who've abandoned Humanity. Each Path redefines what constitutes "sin." Primarily Sabbat. Requires Conviction+Instinct instead of Conscience+Self-Control. ↔ N306
[N356] Path of Blood ◆ {V20} — Assamite path: perfection through blood refinement. Sins: failing to diablerize when possible, showing mercy to unworthy. Virtues: Conviction, Self-Control.
[N357] Path of Bones ◆ {V20} — Death-scholars: understand death's meaning. Sins: failing to study death, accidental killing (wastes data), showing fear of death. Virtues: Conviction, Self-Control. "Graverobbers."
[N358] Path of Caine ◆ {V20} — Noddist scholars: emulate Caine's path. Sins: failing to pursue Caine-lore, denial of vampiric nature, killing other Cainites needlessly. Virtues: Conviction, Instinct.
[N359] Path of Cathari ◆ {V20} — Liberation through transgression: sin frees the soul. Sins: restraining the Beast, refusing pleasure, abstinence. Virtues: Conviction, Instinct.
[N360] Path of Death and the Soul ◆ {V20} — Giovanni variant of Path of Bones: understand death AND the soul's fate. Sins: same as Bones with added soul-study requirements. Virtues: Conviction, Self-Control.
[N361] Path of the Feral Heart ◆ {V20} — Beast-as-guide: embrace predatory nature. Sins: unnecessary cruelty (wastes energy), failure to assert dominance, failure to hunt. Virtues: Conviction, Instinct. ✶?
[N362] Path of Harmony ◆ {V20} — Balance human and Beast: neither suppress nor surrender to the Beast. Sins: Killing without need, losing control to Beast OR suppressing it entirely. Virtues: Conscience, Instinct.
[N363] Path of Honorable Accord ◆ {V20} — Knightly honor code: keep oaths, fight fair, protect the weak. Sins: dishonorable combat, oath-breaking, cowardice. Virtues: Conscience, Self-Control.
[N364] Path of Lilith ◆ {V20} — Suffer to grow: pain-as-enlightenment. Sins: avoiding hardship, refusing to test oneself through suffering, denying others their suffering. Virtues: Conviction, Instinct.
[N365] Path of Metamorphosis ◆ {V20} — Tzimisce self-perfection: transcend human/vampire form. Sins: failing to alter oneself, succumbing to emotion, not experimenting on others. Virtues: Conviction, Instinct.
[N366] Path of Night ◆ {V20} — Lasombra path: embrace darkness and damnation. Sins: showing compassion, promoting hope, failing to be monstrous. Serve as dark instruments of God's judgment. Virtues: Conviction, Instinct.
[N367] Path of Paradox ◆ {V20} — Ravnos path: understand Maya (illusion of reality). Sins: failing to destroy the unworthy, embracing permanence, not pursuing enlightenment. Virtues: Conviction, Self-Control.
[N368] Path of Power and the Inner Voice ◆ {V20} — Social Darwinism: power justifies itself. Sins: failing to seize advantage, submitting to unworthy authority, failing to advance. Virtues: Conviction, Instinct.
[N369] Path of Typhon ◆ {V20} — Setite path: liberate others through corruption. Sins: failing to corrupt, refusing to destroy order, not pursuing Set's agenda. Virtues: Conviction, Self-Control.
